#  Strategic Business Recommendations

##  Promotion Optimization

* Maintain promotion intensity within optimal band (4‚Äì6)
* Avoid extreme promotional saturation

---

##  Discount Strategy

* Focus on mid-range discounts
* Prevent margin erosion from deep discounting

---

##  Marketing Spend Allocation

* Reallocate spend from low-footfall products
* Increase investment in high-elasticity SKUs

---

##  Inventory Planning

* Align stock levels with promotion cycles
* Prevent stockouts during high campaigns
* Avoid overstocking low-demand items

---

##  Pricing Strategy

* Maintain competitive pricing gaps
* Avoid pricing significantly above competitors
* Use price perception to drive sentiment

---

##  Returns Reduction Strategy

* Target low-sentiment customers with service recovery
* Improve product education during promotions
* Tighten return policies for heavily discounted items
